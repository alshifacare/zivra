Our Story

Zeenat started with a simple frustration: real gold jewellery was too expensive to wear every day, and most "artificial" jewellery available in the market tarnished within weeks. So we set out to build a collection that looks premium, feels light, and actually survives daily wear — the office, the dawat, the wedding season, all of it.

What Makes Us Different

Every piece goes through nickel and lead testing before it reaches our warehouse. We use anti-tarnish plating on all our gold and rose-gold finishes, so your jewellery keeps its shine long after the season photos are taken.

Made For Pakistan

Our design team works with tones and silhouettes that suit desi skin tones and occasions — from minimal everyday studs to full jhumka and choker sets for wedding season. We ship to every city in Pakistan, with cash-on-delivery available nationwide.
